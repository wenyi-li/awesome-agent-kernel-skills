## Summary
- Clear stale SWA mappings on non-tail full indices in `alloc_extend_swa_tail`
- Recycled full KV pages may retain mappings from a previous request's `alloc_extend` (1:1 SWA), causing eviction or free to double-free SWA pages belonging to other requests

## Verified

- Without fix: crashes at ~2000 requests with `AssertionError: swa_attn_allocator.available_size() <= swa_attn_allocator.size`
- With fix: 10240 requests completed, 0 errors
