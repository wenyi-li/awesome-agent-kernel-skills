## Summary

## Motivation

\`init_forward_metadata_capture_cuda_graph\` and \`init_forward_metadata_replay_cuda_graph\` share substantial logic across every attention backend but were maintained as independent copies. This caused real divergence: \`WaveAttnBackend\` silently skipped \`get_num_kv_splits\` during capture, \`CutlassMLABackend\` had a spurious \`assert seq_lens_cpu is not None\` (the parameter was never read), and \`FlashInferAttnBackend\` duplicated ~240 lines of per-mode dispatch between the two methods.

## Modifications

**WaveAttnBackend** (Pattern A — capture delegates to replay):
- Add \`_build_cuda_graph_forward_metadata\` to assemble \`ForwardMetadata\` from pre-allocated buffers.
- \`capture\` early-returns for the multi-step spec-decode path, then delegates all buffer writes to \`replay\` and calls \`_build_cuda_graph_forward_metadata\` to freeze the result.
- Fixes a latent bug: \`get_num_kv_splits\` was not called during capture but was called during replay, causing inconsistent state.
